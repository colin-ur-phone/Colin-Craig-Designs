About

Colin Craig is a Graphic Designer and Illustrator with a focus on designing quality logos that generate a positive perception of any business or organisation. After studying a variety of disciplines with the use of online sources he has learned to combine psychology, sales, and graphic design to communicate effectively through the language of visuals. His calm mind and discipline allow him to maintain focus while working to solve challenging creative problems.

Wanderluxe Expeditions

WanderLuxe Expeditions is a fictional travel company that specializes in curating exclusive and adventurous travel experiences. This logo was created using an AI generated brief to simulate challenging design requirements.

Evergreen Eats

Evergreen Eats is a fictional plant-based meal delivery service that offers healthy, sustainable, and delicious meals made from locally sourced, organic ingredients.
